Fantasy hockey keeper / dynasty rankings

Most valuable players for long-term leagues; podcast preview for 2023-24

by Fantasy Hockey Staff @NHLFantasy

NOTES: This list of players and prospects who are 25 years old or younger and should be used to prioritize players for fantasy roster decisions and drafts in keeper and dynasty leagues. Value has been quantified based on factors including but not limited to age, draft position and year, team security, projected long-term lineup placement and more. Some valuable players who are 26 years old or older, most notably Connor McDavid (top player in all fantasy formats), should also be prioritized in keeper and dynasty leagues but have been excluded from this list.
