ICE HOCKEY

ABOUT HOCKEY

What is ice hockey? Ice hockey is a game between two teams who wear skates and compete on an ice rink. It is a 6v6 brutal competition of strength and endurance. Each side consists of 3 forwards and 2 defencement with Goaltender at the back. They play not with a ball but a puck on the ice that gets shot and with sticks made for Ice Hockey - Some shots can come off the stick at 180 km/h -Thats fast ! Each team has 20 players :

  • 4 lines of 3 players - Forwards
  • 3 lines of 2 players - Defence
  • 2 Goal Keepers - One backup

”A Great Hockey Player Plays Where The Puck Is Going To Be” – Wayne Gretzky

RULES OF HOCKEY

The rules of hockey can be quite intimidating but are actually pretty logical and are picked up very quickly to name a few :

  • Icing - This is when the puck is shot from one end of the rink to the other the whistle is blown and the puck is brough back
  • Offside - This is when a player crosses the offesive blue line before the puck
  • Delay of game - This is when the puck is shot out of the area on purpose or by mistake when in the defensive zone
